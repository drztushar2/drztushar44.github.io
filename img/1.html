Welcome TO ACR RENDERING !

QUALITY IS OUR FIRST PRIORITY

Having residents of Australia at the helm of its affairs, ACR Rendering provides astonishingly remarkable services for Acrylic and Cement Rendering, Internal and External Plastering and Coating, Wall Cladding, Texture Coatings, and Painting across Dandenong and overall Melbourne.

Our trained and certified craftsmen have extensive and diverse experience in new construction projects, home & office renovations, as well as repair and maintenance of buildings.

  • We have a license and insurance coverage for all the activities that we perform.
  • Whether it’s a small project or big, our prices for all services are very competitive.
  • Our experts suggest solutions considering your specific needs and budget.
  • We plan in advance and account for uncertainties as well to meet your objectives.

The clients we have served till now, always recommend us to their acquaintances, searching for a reputable firm for residential and commercial rendering in Dandenong and surrounding South East Suburbs of Melbourne.
